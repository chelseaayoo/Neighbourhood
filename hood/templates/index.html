24/7 DIGITAL ESTATE UNDER SURVEILLANCE

ADMINISTRATION

The chief's office is located at the right hand side of the estate's entrance. Any new resident must report to the chief's office after signing in to the estate's website.

HOUSING

Ardhi house is located beside the chief's office. Anyone who wishes to set up any amenities in the estate must report to the office for clearance.Any land and border disputes in the estate must be reported to the office.

BUSINESS

Business operations are only permitted at our market place, Arina Centre, located just before the estate's hind entrance.

The Revenue Authority's office is located at the market and one is required to report to the office for clearance after the online business registration.
